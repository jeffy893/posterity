# Philosophical Works & Social Theory

Theoretical frameworks and philosophical explorations addressing contemporary social, economic, and political challenges.

## Core Theoretical Works

### Dunbar Theory & Social Dynamics
- **Dunbar Calculus of Value** - Mathematical framework for human social capacity and value creation
- **Dunbar Bowls Dissertation Outline** - Academic framework for social network analysis
- **Dignity on Both Sides** - Conflict resolution and mutual respect frameworks

### Social & Political Philosophy
- **Skepticism of Prioritization** - Critical analysis of decision-making hierarchies
- **Common Grievances** - Constitutional and governance analysis
- **Bylaws on Indemnifications** - Legal and organizational theory
- **Testimony About Current Leadership** - Political and social commentary

### Behavioral Economics & Psychology
- **Prospect Theory of Mating and Dating** - Economic models applied to human relationships
- **Queens Gambit** - Strategic thinking and decision-making analysis
- **Juice Worth the Squeeze** - Cost-benefit analysis in human endeavors

### Creative Philosophy
- **Gracers Screenplay** - Narrative exploration of social themes
- **West African Romeo and Juliet** - Cross-cultural adaptation and universal themes
- **Tired of Vanity** - Critique of superficiality and authentic living
- **Hermes** - Mythological and archetypal analysis

## Philosophical Themes

These works address:
- **Social capacity limits** and the mathematics of human relationships (Dunbar's Number)
- **Constitutional principles** and the balance of individual vs. collective rights
- **Economic behavior** and the psychology of decision-making
- **Conflict resolution** and the preservation of dignity in disagreement
- **Cultural adaptation** and universal human experiences
- **Authentic living** versus societal expectations and vanity
